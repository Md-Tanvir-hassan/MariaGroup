Maria Enterprise: Trusted Partner of JICA in Bangladesh

Collaboration with JICA reflects our commitment to delivering premium quality services and materials

Maria Enterprise is proud to be a reliable partner of the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) in Bangladesh, contributing to the success of their various development projects across the country.

Our collaboration with JICA reflects our commitment to delivering premium quality services and materials to support large-scale infrastructure and development initiatives.

Our Services to JICA

Maria Enterprise provides comprehensive solutions to support JICA's development projects across Bangladesh

🚗

Vehicle Supply & Rental Services

One of the key services we provide to JICA is vehicle supply, rental, and sales to meet the transportation needs of their high-ranking officials, engineers, and project staff.

From executive vehicles for official duties to practical transportation solutions for on-site teams, Maria Enterprise ensures safety, reliability, and comfort.

Our Fleet Includes:

  • Executive Sedans
  • SUVs for Field Operations
  • Pickup Trucks
  • Specialized Vehicles

All maintained to international standards to guarantee smooth operations for JICA's ongoing projects in Bangladesh.

🏗️

Construction Materials Supply

In addition to transportation solutions, Maria Enterprise is a trusted supplier of high-quality construction materials required for JICA-funded projects.

Our focus is on timely delivery and maintaining the highest quality standards to align with JICA's mission of sustainable development in Bangladesh.

Materials We Supply:

  • Sylhet Sand
  • Premium-Grade Wood
  • High-Quality Cement
  • Construction Aggregates

Supporting infrastructure, road construction, and housing projects across Bangladesh.
