BNS Project Overview

Maria Groups' Strategic Contribution to the BNS Project, Pekua – Cox's Bazar

Our Role in National Defense Infrastructure

Maria Groups is honored to be playing a vital role in the development of the Bangladesh Navy's strategic base at Pekua, Cox's Bazar. As part of this landmark national project, our company has been entrusted with multiple responsibilities that contribute to the infrastructure, operational readiness, and overall success of the BNS (Bangladesh Navy Submarine Base) Pekua establishment.

Comprehensive Supply and Construction Support

With our reputation for reliability, quality assurance, and logistics excellence, Maria Groups has been awarded several contracts at BNS Pekua, spanning across various domains of material supply and construction support.

1

Supply of Wine Wood in Large Volumes

One of our primary roles has been the procurement and supply of high-grade wine wood in bulk quantities. This timber is being used extensively in structural frameworks, interiors, and specialized naval facilities within the base. Our supply chain management ensures timely delivery, consistent quality, and compliance with all naval construction standards.

2

Construction Materials for Infrastructure Development

Maria Groups has consistently supplied a broad spectrum of essential building materials required for the development of the naval base. This includes but is not limited to:

  • High-strength cement
  • Rebars and steel reinforcements
  • Bricks and concrete blocks
  • Sand and aggregates
  • Waterproofing materials

These materials have supported the construction of residential quarters, command centers, training facilities, storage depots, and other critical infrastructure.

3

Tiles and Interior Finishing

We have also supplied premium-quality floor and wall tiles for use across various sectors of the naval base. Whether it's officers' quarters, administrative offices, or recreational buildings, our carefully curated selection of tiles enhances both functionality and aesthetic appeal.

4

Paint and Coating Works

Our company has delivered a wide range of painting solutions, including weather-resistant exterior paints and hygienic, washable interior coatings. In addition to supplying, we have been involved in paint application work as per the specific guidelines set by the engineering department of the Bangladesh Navy, ensuring a lasting finish and protective coverage for buildings within the base.

5

Specialized Equipment and Logistic Solutions

Beyond construction, Maria Groups has been responsible for sourcing and delivering various project-specific materials and tools required by different wings of the base. Our experience in handling government and defense-related contracts allows us to operate with precision, discretion, and adherence to protocols.

A Trusted Partner in National Development

Our involvement in the BNS Pekua project is a testament to Maria Groups' growing presence in the infrastructure and defense support sector. We are proud to contribute to a project that strengthens national security while creating opportunities for local economic growth in the Cox's Bazar region.

As we continue our commitment to excellence, Maria Groups looks forward to deepening our engagement with government and military agencies, delivering quality, consistency, and innovation across every contract we undertake.
