Message from Chairman

Visionary Leadership Guiding Maria Groups

[SYED MOAZZEM HOSSAIN ALAL]
Chairman
Maria Groups

To Our Dedicated Team, Trusted Partners, and Cherished Friends,

It is both an honor and a responsibility to address you at a time when our organization stands as a proven leader in business—driven by opportunity, powered by innovation, and guided by a clear sense of purpose.

At Maria Groups, we believe that true progress begins with people. A respectful, inclusive, and growth-oriented work environment is not just a corporate policy—it is our foundation. We are committed to nurturing a culture where every voice matters, every contribution counts, and every individual is empowered to thrive. The strength of our people defines the strength of our company.

We are also deeply aware of the larger ecosystem in which we operate. Bangladesh is on a promising path of economic transformation, driven by innovation, industrial diversification, and a vibrant entrepreneurial spirit. As proud contributors to this journey, Maria Groups continues to expand its presence across sectors—from agriculture to technology, from logistics to infrastructure—with a clear focus on value creation and sustainability.

"Success must never be one-dimensional. Our vision extends beyond profit margins and market share. We believe in doing business with conscience, in creating impact with integrity."

But success must never be one-dimensional. Our vision extends beyond profit margins and market share. We believe in doing business with conscience, in creating impact with integrity. Whether through our work in rural communities, food security programs, or green energy projects, we are committed to being a responsible force for good—both locally and globally.

To our employees, I encourage you to be fearless in your ambitions and grounded in your values. To our clients and partners, I extend sincere thanks for your trust and collaboration. And to our broader community, I reaffirm our pledge to stand by the principles of fairness, transparency, and shared progress.

Let us continue this journey together—with bold vision, compassionate leadership, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

With warm regards,
[SYED MOAZZEM HOSSAIN ALAL]
Chairman, Maria Groups
