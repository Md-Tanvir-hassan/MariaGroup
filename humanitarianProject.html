Humanitarian Initiative

40-Bed Specialized Hospital Construction Project

Ukhiya, Cox's Bazar

Maria Groups is honored to participate in the construction of a 40-bed specialized hospital in Ukhiya, Cox's Bazar—an essential healthcare facility being developed by Save the Children to serve both the host community and Rohingya refugees in the region.

Our Scope of Work

Our company has been entrusted with a wide-ranging scope of work, contributing significantly to the hospital's successful implementation.

🏗️

Structural Support & Civil Construction

Supplying reinforced concrete materials and overseeing structural framework to ensure safety and durability.

⚡

Electrical Installation

Complete setup of power distribution, wiring, lighting systems, and backup generator integration.

🚰

Plumbing & Sanitation

End-to-end installation of water supply, drainage, and sanitation infrastructure in compliance with health standards.

🎨

Interior & Finishing Works

High-quality interior fittings, tiling, painting, and general finishing work tailored to a medical facility's needs.

🚚

Logistics & Material Supply

Consistent and timely delivery of construction materials, fixtures, and fittings, sourced from trusted partners.
