About Maria Groups

A Legacy of Trust. A Future of Possibilities.

Overview – "Maria Groups"

Founded with a vision to transform industries and enrich lives, Maria Groups is a multi-disciplinary conglomerate based in Bangladesh with a rapidly expanding presence across local and international markets. We operate at the intersection of innovation, quality, and integrity — proudly serving as a Producer, Processor, Agriculturist, Contractor, Supplier, Importer, and Exporter across diverse sectors.

With a commitment to national development and global excellence, Maria Groups integrates agriculture, manufacturing, construction, technology, trade, and logistics to deliver end-to-end solutions that meet the evolving demands of our clients and communities. From producing safe and sustainable food products to executing large-scale construction projects and facilitating global trade, our operations are designed to create long-term value.

Our Core Business Verticals:

Agriculture & Food Processing
Leveraging sustainable practices and modern technology to cultivate, process, and supply quality agro-based products for domestic and global markets.
Construction & Contracting
Delivering turnkey infrastructure and civil engineering solutions that contribute to national development and urban progress.
Trade & Supply Chain
Serving as a trusted supplier, importer, and exporter of a wide range of commodities and products, with a strong network across Asia, the Middle East, and beyond.
Software & Technology
Enabling digital transformation through innovative software development and IT-enabled services.
